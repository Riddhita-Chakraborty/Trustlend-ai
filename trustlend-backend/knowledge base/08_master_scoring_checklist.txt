LOAN DOCUMENT ANALYSIS — MASTER SCORING CHECKLIST
=======================================================================

HOW TO USE:
- When analysing a loan document, check for each item below
- Assign point deductions as indicated
- Start from 100, deduct for each red flag found
- Minimum score: 0

=======================================================================
CATEGORY 1: INTEREST RATE (Max deduction: 40 points)
=======================================================================
[ ] Is the interest rate stated clearly in % per annum?
    NO: -5 points

[ ] Is the method stated (flat rate or reducing balance)?
    NOT STATED: Assume flat rate and flag — effective rate is nearly double
    FLAT RATE (undisclosed): -10 points additional

[ ] Interest rate against Indian benchmarks:
    PERSONAL LOAN:
    ≤14%:   0 points
    14-20%: -5 points
    20-28%: -20 points
    >28%:   -40 points

    HOME LOAN:
    ≤10%:   0 points
    10-13%: -10 points
    13-16%: -20 points
    >16%:   -40 points

    GENERAL/UNKNOWN LOAN TYPE:
    ≤15%:   0 points
    15-20%: -10 points
    20-30%: -25 points
    >30%:   -40 points

[ ] For floating rate: Is benchmark specified (MCLR/Repo rate)?
    NOT SPECIFIED: -20 points
    SPECIFIED: 0 points

[ ] "Rate at lender's discretion" language present?
    YES: -20 points

=======================================================================
CATEGORY 2: FEES AND CHARGES (Max deduction: 35 points)
=======================================================================
[ ] Processing fee:
    Not disclosed: -10 points
    Disclosed and ≤2%: 0 points
    Disclosed and 2-4%: -5 points
    >4% of loan: -15 points

[ ] APR (Annual Percentage Rate) disclosed?
    NO APR stated: -15 points
    APR stated including all charges: 0 points

[ ] KFS summary box present at start of document?
    NO KFS: -20 points
    KFS present with all fields: 0 points

[ ] Hidden or undisclosed charges suspected?
    YES ("including but not limited to", "subject to charges"): -10 points

[ ] Third-party charges (insurance, legal, valuation) included in APR?
    NOT INCLUDED despite being mandatory: -10 points

=======================================================================
CATEGORY 3: PENAL CHARGES (Max deduction: 25 points)
=======================================================================
[ ] Old-style "penal interest added to rate" (non-compliant post Jan 2024)?
    YES: -25 points (RBI circular violation)

[ ] Penal charges applied on entire outstanding balance (not just overdue)?
    YES: -15 points

[ ] Penal charges are compounded?
    YES: -20 points (RBI violation)

[ ] Penal charges disclosed clearly in loan document?
    NOT DISCLOSED: -10 points

[ ] Penal charges proportionate?
    >5% per month on overdue: -15 points

=======================================================================
CATEGORY 4: RISKY CLAUSES (Max deduction: 60 points)
=======================================================================
[ ] Mandatory arbitration clause removing court/consumer forum access?
    YES: -15 points

[ ] Borrower waives fundamental rights (notice, objection, legal recourse)?
    YES: -15 to -20 points

[ ] Unilateral acceleration clause (lender can demand full repayment at will)?
    YES: -15 points

[ ] Cross-default clause present?
    YES: -10 points

[ ] Balloon payment (undisclosed or disproportionate)?
    YES: -15 points

[ ] Negative amortization terms?
    YES: -25 points

[ ] Unilateral modification of loan terms by lender?
    YES: -10 points

[ ] Prepayment penalty on individual floating rate loan?
    YES: -15 points (RBI violation)

[ ] Excessive prepayment penalty (>3% or applies beyond year 3)?
    YES: -10 points

[ ] Forced insurance not included in APR?
    YES: -15 points

[ ] Recovery via contact to family/employer authorised in document?
    YES: -15 points

=======================================================================
CATEGORY 5: DISCLOSURE AND TRANSPARENCY (Max deduction: 30 points)
=======================================================================
[ ] Borrower receives full copy of agreement?
    NOT STATED / DENIED: -10 points

[ ] All terms in writing and certified?
    NOT MENTIONED: -5 points

[ ] Changes to terms require advance notice to borrower?
    NOT MENTIONED: -10 points

[ ] Security release timeline on full repayment specified?
    NOT SPECIFIED: -5 points

[ ] Grievance redressal mechanism with contact provided?
    NOT PROVIDED: -10 points

[ ] RBI Ombudsman escalation path mentioned?
    NOT MENTIONED: -5 points

[ ] Interest charged from actual disbursement date (not sanction date)?
    SANCTION DATE USED: -15 points (RBI violation)

=======================================================================
VERDICT TABLE
=======================================================================
SCORE 80-100: FAIR
    Document appears compliant with RBI guidelines.
    No major red flags detected.
    Borrower can proceed with reasonable confidence.

SCORE 50-79: NEEDS REVIEW
    Some concerning clauses or disclosure gaps detected.
    Recommend borrower seek legal/financial advice before signing.
    Ask lender to clarify flagged items.

SCORE 0-49: UNFAIR
    Multiple severe red flags detected.
    Potential RBI guideline violations.
    High risk of hidden costs or loss of borrower rights.
    STRONGLY recommend legal review and/or complaint to RBI Ombudsman.

COMMON LOAN TYPES IN INDIA — QUICK CONTEXT
=======================================================================
Home Loan:       Secured. Typically 15-30 years. EBLR/MCLR linked. Floating rate = no prepayment penalty.
Personal Loan:   Unsecured. 1-5 years. Higher rates justified. Watch hidden charges.
Auto Loan:       Secured (vehicle). 3-7 years. Watch for insurance bundling.
Gold Loan:       Secured (gold). 3-12 months. Very short term, watch for balloon payment.
Education Loan:  Long tenure, moratorium period common. Watch for unclear interest terms during moratorium.
MSME Loan:       Business purpose. Can be secured or unsecured. Floating rate = no prepayment penalty.
Microfinance:    Small unsecured loans, often weekly/fortnightly repayment. RBI caps margins.
Digital Loan:    Short tenure, online. Highest risk for hidden charges and data misuse.
